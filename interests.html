I'm mainly interested in two questions:

  1. what a sustainable and just society looks like, and
  2. how to get there.

Or, to put it another way, how to enable everyone to live well without f***ing the planet.

So just the small things, really.

My focus atm is on the role technology could/should play within that, and on what more effective forms of governance for tackling complex challenges might look like.

I'm currently using my makeshift blog to keep track of my thoughts and throw ideas around, on these issues and others (including books, geopolitics, knowledge/info management - yes, I know, I'm sad - and music).

I also go through waves of tweeting about stuff as @lewisalloyd.
